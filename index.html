The Crystal Gems!!!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four gems below. By clicking on a gem you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the "Number to Guess," and you lose the game if your total score goes above that "Number to Guess."

The value of each gem is unknown from you until you click on it!

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each gem.
